Customer Retention & Performance Analysis

Business Problem
How can a company identify the key drivers of customer retention and reduce churn to improve long-term performance?

Approach

Analyzed customer data across engagement, purchase behaviour, and subscription usage
Focused on understanding what drives retention vs churn
Evaluated how customer interactions influence long-term loyalty

Key Insights

Email engagement strongly drives retention
Subscription services increase customer loyalty
Customer behaviour predicts churn risk
Retention is driven more by engagement than transaction size

Business Impact

Identified high-value customer behaviours to prioritize
Provided recommendations to improve retention and customer engagement
Supported data-driven decision-making
